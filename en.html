What Alfred can do

Conveniently manage your stay and enjoy everything offered by hotel, all thanks to Alfred.

  • Hotel and surroundings
  • Additional services
  • Check-in/out
  • Payment
  • Review
  • Lock solution

Hotel and surroundings

You simply sign into the application using the PIN that you receive with a confirmation email.

Alfred helps not only with accommodation, but also offers tips for activities. From monuments, restaurants, attractions to specific trips and cultural events.

Additional services

What about breakfast to your room, a massage or a bike rental. Hotel services can be easily booked by Alfred from anywhere.

Check-in/out

Don't waste your time at the front desk. You can enter your personal data to Alfred and he will do the check-in for you. You can head straight to your room.

You can also easily check-out with Alfred and leave without waiting at front desk.

Payment

You can pay for your stay and services easily with Alfred's help. Upon request, he will give you a bill that you can pay directly online.

Review

Your feedback is the most valuable thing Alfred can take away from your stay. At the end of your stay you can easily write review in the app. Every suggestion or commentary is vital, because only that way hotel can keep improving.

Lock solution

Alfred is able to open the door for you at selected accommodation facilities. Just one click on your phone and you're in the comfort of your room. Forget the cards and keys, you have Alfred.
